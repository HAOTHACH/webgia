devices


Responsive design

With a mobile focus, you can rest assured that every pixel will respond as it should no matter if your visitors are on a desktop, tablet or phone.

Improper and depercated code can break layouts, corrupt data or worse. With Dannel, nothing but the latest version of HTML5 and CSS3 are used.
